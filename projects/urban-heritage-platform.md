# Urban Heritage Exploration Platform

**Lead UX Designer · Research Collaborator**
Digital Heritage Lab, New York University (NYU) Shanghai · May – September 2025

---

## Overview

An interactive platform for exploring urban heritage sites, designed in collaboration with academic and industry partners at NYU Shanghai's Digital Heritage Lab. The platform combines game design principles with cultural storytelling to support audience engagement with living heritage.

---

## Responsibilities

### UX/UI Design

Designed and implemented the full UX/UI flow — from initial wireframes through to functional prototype integration. Worked directly with developers to translate design into working interfaces.

### Research & Methodology

Applied ethnographic and participatory design methods to centre community knowledge in the platform's architecture. Informed design decisions through user research and iterative feedback.

### Game Design Principles

Drew on interactive narrative and game design frameworks to structure how users move through and interpret heritage content — balancing informational depth with experiential engagement.
